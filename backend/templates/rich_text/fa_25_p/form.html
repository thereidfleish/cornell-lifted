Lifted Fall 2025


We're back for Fall 2025! Show your appreciation and lift some spirits!


Fill out the form here to send a thank you message to someone who has lifted you at Cornell. Your message will be posted on a notecard (for your recipient to keep!) in the Willard Straight Hall Lobby on the last day of classes (Monday, December 8th), along with a sweet treat and winter lights to celebrate the end of the semester.


The form will close either after 2,000 recipients (due to space constraints) or on Sunday, November 30th at 11:59 PM! Any messages submitted after will be sent virtually to your recipient.


Check us out at our Instagram @cornelllifted and read more about Lifted


If you have any questions, check out our FAQs or feel free to contact us at lifted@cornell.edu


Note: To send a Lifted message through this form, you need to sign in with your Cornell NetID. If you don't have one (e.g., you're a parent), send us an email and we'll send your message for you :)

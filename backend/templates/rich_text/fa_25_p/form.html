Lifted Spring 2025


We're back for Spring 2025! Show your appreciation and lift some spirits!


Fill out the form here to send a thank you message to someone who has lifted you at Cornell. Your message will be posted on a notecard (for your recipient to keep!) on the Arts Quad on the last day of classes (Tuesday, May 6th), along with a balloon, flower, pinwheel, or more to celebrate the end of the semester.


The form will close on Friday, April 25th at 11:59 PM! Any messages submitted after will be sent virtually to your recipient.


Check us out at our Instagram @cornelllifted and read more about Lifted


If you have any questions, check out our FAQs or feel free to contact us at lifted@cornell.edu


Note: To send a Lifted message through this form, you need to sign in with your Cornell NetID. If you don't have one (e.g., you're a parent), send us an email and we'll send your message for you :)

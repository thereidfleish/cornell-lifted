Lifted Spring 2026

We're back for Spring 2026! Show your appreciation and lift some spirits!

Fill out the form here to send a thank you message to someone who has lifted you at Cornell.

Lifted is changing this Spring

By default, your card will be delivered to your recipient virtually on the last day of classes (Tuesday, May 5th). However, your recipient can choose to receive a physical copy of their card (for them to keep!) on the Arts Quad, along with a balloon, flower, or pinwheel, to celebrate the end of the semester.

The form will close on Thursday, April 26th at 11:59 PM! Any messages submitted after will only be sent virtually to your recipient.

Check us out at our Instagram @cornelllifted and read more about Lifted

If you have any questions, check out our FAQs or feel free to contact us at lifted@cornell.edu

Note: To send a Lifted message through this form, you need to sign in with your Cornell NetID. If you don't have one (e.g., you're a parent), send us an email and we'll send your message for you :)

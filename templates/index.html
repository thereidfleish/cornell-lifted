About Lifted

Lifted is a fully student-run annual (and perhaps going forward, semesterly!) tradition where Cornellians come together to lift the spirits of their friends, faculty, and staff at Cornell on the last day of classes! It's a time to express gratitude, spread positivity, and watch Cornell transform into a vibrant landscape of heartfelt messages on the last day of classes. This uplifting event is a beautiful reminder of the connections we've made, the support we've received, and the unforgettable moments we've shared each semester.

Lifted was created in Spring 2016, and since 2018, {{ "{:,}".format(stats["unique_sent"]) }} individual Cornellians have Lifted {{ "{:,}".format(stats["unique_received"]) }} individual fellow classmates, faculty, and staff, sending a total of {{ "{:,}".format(stats["total_received"]) }} messages!

Lifted is made possible through the generous support of SAFC and our supporters through Cornell Giving Day.

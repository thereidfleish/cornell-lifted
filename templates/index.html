Get ready to lift the spirits of your favorite friends, faculty, and staff at Cornell as we come together for our end-of-semester Lifted tradition! It's time to express gratitude, spread positivity, and watch Cornell transform into a vibrant landscape of heartfelt messages.

On the last day of classes, each personalized message will be printed and displayed amongst thousands of messages. In the Spring, cards will be attached to a cheerful balloon or flower on the Arts Quad, and in the Fall, cards will be hung from dazzling lights in Willard Straight Hall.

Lifted Fall 2024


Surprise! We're back for Fall 2024, our first time during a Fall semester! Show your appreciation and lift some spirits!


Fill out the form here to send a thank you message to someone who has lifted you at Cornell. Messages will be displayed in Willard Straight Hall to be picked up on the last day of classes (Monday, December 9th), along with some sweet treats and winter lights! ❄️


The form will close either after 2,000 submissions (due to space constraints) or on Sunday, December 1st at 11:59 PM! Any messages submitted after will be sent virtually to your recipient.


Instagram: @cornelllifted

YouTube feature 2021: youtube.com/watch?v=ewJ3UY5mTYA


If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at lifted@cornell.edu


Note: In order to send a Lifted message through this form, you need to sign in with your Cornell NetID. If you don't have one (e.g., you're a parent), send us an email and we'll send your message for you :)
